Events by Carrie

Carrie Rogers · Event Design & Production · Research Triangle, NC

Name rationale

"Events by Carrie" is personal and direct — her name is the brand promise. It's warm enough for a nonprofit cultivation dinner and professional enough for a corporate milestone. The italic treatment on "Carrie" in headlines gives it a signature quality, like a handwritten sign-off.

Primary tagline

"You bring the vision. We bring the experience."

Alternates

Every detail. Every guest. Every time. Events that feel as good as they look. From first email to final toast.
